Privacy Policy

At Billionaire Bridge, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all personal information collected from our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when opening an account, subscribing to services, or communicating with us. This includes your name, contact details, financial information, identity documents, and trading history. We use this information to facilitate account management, process transactions, comply with regulatory requirements including anti-money laundering laws, and to provide you with personalized investment services.

Your data is stored securely using advanced encryption technologies and access controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted service providers, regulatory bodies, or legal authorities as required by law. You have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.

Regular reviews and updates to our data protection practices ensure your information is handled with the highest standards of security and confidentiality, reflecting our commitment to your privacy.
